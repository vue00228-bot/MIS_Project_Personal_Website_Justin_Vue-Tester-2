About Me

I'm Justin Vue, a Retail Associate with about two years of experience in customer service and sales. I'm currently pursuing a Finance major in college, with the goal of owning my own business one day.

My Journey

From working at Chipotle to Walmart and now UMD Dining Services, I've learned the importance of teamwork, efficiency, and attention to detail. Each role has taught me valuable skills about customer service and operations management.

Goals

My ultimate goal is to own my own business. I'm building my skills in finance and business management to make this dream a reality. I believe in continuous learning and hard work.

Why I Chose UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because it's close enough to home to stay connected with family, yet far enough to experience independence. Originally, I was interested in their Civil Engineering program, but even though I'm no longer pursuing that path, I've grown to appreciate the scenery and the welcoming community on campus.

What I Like About Duluth and the Surrounding Area

I really enjoy the natural beauty and scenic views around Duluth. The combination of lakes, forests, and outdoor spaces makes it a great place to explore and relax.

Personal Experiences and Campus Highlights

One of my favorite experiences at UMD was emceeing for HLUB's Hmong New Year and Heritage Night events. Being part of these celebrations allowed me to connect with the community, celebrate my culture, and take on a leadership role on campus.
